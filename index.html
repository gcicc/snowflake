Snowflake Grower

Watch a unique ice crystal grow from a single seed. No two snowflakes are alike — each one is generated with randomized branching, just like real crystallization.

Branches controls the number of arms (real snowflakes have 6). Complexity controls how many sub-branches grow — like how temperature and humidity affect real crystal formation.

Try the presets: Dendrite (classic snowflake), Plate (simple hexagon), Fern (heavily branched), Star (medium detail).

Tap New Crystal to grow another — each one will be different. Tap Immerse to watch full-screen.
